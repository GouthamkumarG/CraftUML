@startuml
participant "Calendarservice" as Calendarservice
participant "Clinicapp" as Clinicapp
participant "Patient" as Patient
participant "Paymentgateway" as Paymentgateway

Patient -> Clinicapp: Book Appointment
Clinicapp -> Calendarservice: Check Doctor Availability
Calendarservice -> Clinicapp: Available Slots
Patient -> Clinicapp: Confirm Slot
Clinicapp -> Paymentgateway: Process Payment
Paymentgateway -> Clinicapp: Payment Confirmed
Clinicapp -> Patient: Appointment Confirmation
@enduml
