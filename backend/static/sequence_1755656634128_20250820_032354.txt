@startuml
participant "Inventorysystem" as Inventorysystem
participant "Onlinestore" as Onlinestore
participant "Paymentgateway" as Paymentgateway
participant "Shopper" as Shopper

Shopper -> Onlinestore: Add Item to Cart
Onlinestore -> Inventorysystem: Check Stock
Inventorysystem -> Onlinestore: Stock Available
Shopper -> Onlinestore: Proceed to Checkout
Onlinestore -> Paymentgateway: Process Payment
Paymentgateway -> Onlinestore: Payment Approved
Onlinestore -> Shopper: Order Confirmation
@enduml
